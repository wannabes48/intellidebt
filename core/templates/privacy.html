Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Intellidebt collects financial data purely for the purpose of predictive analysis. This includes loan history, repayment dates, and basic borrower demographics provided by your institution.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to train and run machine learning models. We do not sell or share borrower data with third parties. All analysis is performed within our secure, encrypted environment.

3. Data Security

We implement bank-grade 256-bit encryption for all stored data. Access is restricted via Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) defined by your organization's administrators.

Contact privacy@intellidebt.ai for data deletion requests.
