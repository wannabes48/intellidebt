About Intellidebt

We are on a mission to revolutionize credit management in Africa using ethical, predictive artificial intelligence.

Our Vision

Intellidebt was born out of a simple observation: mid-sized financial institutions like Saccos and MFIs spend too much time chasing debt and not enough time building relationships. We built a system that bridges that gap.

By leveraging Random Forest machine learning models, we analyze historical borrower behavior to predict defaults before they happen, allowing lenders to intervene early with automated, empathetic recovery strategies.

Why AI?

Traditional credit scoring is reactive. It tells you what happened in the past. Intellidebt is proactive. Our models weight dozens of features—from income-to-loan ratios to missed payment frequency—to provide a real-time risk score for every single loan in your portfolio.
